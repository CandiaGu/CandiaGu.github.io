Teaching

Although I have not had children, their uncensored responses to life fascinates me - which is why I work with them often, both because I enjoy their company and because I truly want to enrich their lives with answers to questions they haven’t even thought of. I’ve worked as a tutor, supervisor and camp counselor during past years, teaching them basic school knowledge like math and science and other information such as social behavior to make other people like you (though I haven’t quite mastered this one yet laughs). Overall, I think the way children think greatly impacts the way I view the world—with fascination and great joy.
